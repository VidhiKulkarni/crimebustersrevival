<h1>Learn Caesar!</h1>

What is Caesar?
- A type of decryption method used to hide messages
- Used in around 100 BC by Julius Caesar

How does it work?
- A shift cipher
- All letters shift by 3 to the right. For example, a would become d and b would become e.
